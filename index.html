Welcome to WCC

A family of believers living free and light in Christ and sharing this life with our world


We are so glad you found us! As our mission statement states, WCC is family. We are people with relationships as new as a few weeks and as established as several decades. What we all have in common:
  • Our lives have been radically changed by the grace of God
  • We've had it with the church treadmill of figuring out what we should do for God
  • We're learning the freedom of living based on what Jesus has done (and finished) for us
  • We're blossoming into the kind of life that comes from realizing that God loves us, likes us, and fully accepts us because he has decided we are loveable, likeable, and acceptable (just like you!)
  • We are experiencing the free and light life only possible through our saviour Jesus Christ
Come join us on this journey!
